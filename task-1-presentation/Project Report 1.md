# Project Report: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

Name: Dev Prabhakar Barman 
Registration Number: 25MIP10145  
Institution: Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT)  

For this project, I utilized Canva to design a professional-level infographic focused on Digital Literacy Awareness, specifically tailored to guide first-year students. The design serves as a comprehensive visual roadmap, structurally broken down into five core sections. It begins by clearly defining what digital literacy entails, followed by a curated layout of useful digital tools for academic productivity, research, and collaboration. Additionally, the infographic emphasizes safe internet practices—such as recognizing phishing scams and utilizing two-factor authentication. It also outlines actionable steps for building a solid professional online presence, particularly on platforms like LinkedIn, and concludes with clear guidelines for proper email etiquette when communicating with professors.

One interesting challenge I encountered while creating this was balancing information density with visual clarity. Because digital literacy encompasses such a broad spectrum of topics, condensing complex concepts into digestible, engaging visuals without cluttering the page was initially difficult. However, experimenting with Canva’s grid layouts and cohesive icon sets ultimately helped me achieve a clean, professional aesthetic that is both highly informative and easy to read.
