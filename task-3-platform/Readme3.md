#Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz

Welcome to the Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz project. This assessment was developed as part of a first-year initiative to promote essential digital survival skills, safe internet practices, and professional online communication among university students.

#About the Project
This project features a 5-question interactive quiz designed to test fundamental concepts such as:
* Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) and account security.
* Identifying phishing scams and malicious emails.
* Utilizing reliable digital sources for academic research.
* Practicing professional email etiquette.
* Managing a positive digital footprint and online presence.
